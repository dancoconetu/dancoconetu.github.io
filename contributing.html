Major Contributing Factors to the Accident

Within the 5 boroughs of the New York City, the different type of the factors causing the vehicle collisions are presented accordingly.
We can see that the Driver inattention/Distraction take the majority in all the boroughs with more than 10% for each of them. And followed by Failure to Yield Right-of-Way and Fatigued/Drowsy mostly.
We can clearly see that the reason cause the accidents are mainly because of the driver inattention / distraction. The data is represented in terms of reasons of accidents for each borough.
