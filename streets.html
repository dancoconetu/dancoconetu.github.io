Streets from each Borough which has the Highest Problems

In this plot aims to show which streets in which boroughs have a higher accident rates. Due to representing the data in such a clear format, we can therefore directly show which streets and roads have higher accidents and so isolate the major problem roads. With this view it can be clearly presented to show that these roads require major presence of a better traffic and accident management system.

If we take the example of Hylan Boulervard from Staten's island which seems to have the highest accident rate, this seems mainly because it is one of the major roads in Staten's Island with it covering almost 14 miles or 23km in length. Therefore we can safely assume the larger the length of the road, it would have a relation with the number of accidents encountered being larger. Also with having major intersections with other major roads like New Dorp Lane, which is specifically known for how busy it is, and therefore can attribute to large number of accidents in this area.
